Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our product review summarizer. Our FAQ section covers account management, feature usage, pricing, and technical troubleshooting to help you resolve issues quickly.

How accurate are your review summaries?
Our AI algorithm has been trained on millions of verified product reviews and achieves over 95% accuracy in sentiment analysis and key point extraction.

Can I summarize reviews from any website?
Yes, our tool works with most major e-commerce platforms including Amazon, eBay, Walmart, and thousands of other online retailers.

Is there a limit to how many products I can analyze?
Free accounts can analyze up to 10 products per month. Premium subscribers enjoy unlimited product analysis and additional features.

How do I export or share summaries?
All summaries can be downloaded as PDF reports, shared via direct link, or embedded into your own website with our custom widget.
